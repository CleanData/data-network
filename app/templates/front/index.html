For Data Scientists:
Discover datasets.
Be discovered.

You know the story - you spend an hour searching for a dataset, download it, spend another hour trying to unpack the proprietary data format and then lose a day figuring out why a month of data was overwritten with raw html true story. You're not alone. Everyone using that data has done the same thing. Why not skip the pain and just use their clean data? Because you couldn't find it - until now. Find clean data - and share your own and get credit.

For Industry:
Save time.
Sell data.

Your analysts waste hours cleaning datasets - data that's already been cleaned in another company, government department or academic lab. Use Clean.Data. to discover cleaned datasets and save days. Clean.Data. datasets don't have to be public and open source - they can be proprietary. The network of data shows where your datasets have been used. After all - if 100s of researchers are using your data it's easier for the 101st to decide to buy it.
